About ROOT ACCESS

ROOT ACCESS is a new competitive esports project focused on Delta Force Mobile Attack & Defend. Our goal is to build a disciplined, mature, objective-focused team that values communication, teamwork, consistency, and improvement.

We are not just chasing kill counts. We are building a roster that plays smart, plays together, and represents the team the right way.

Founded by Nomad — content creator, competitive player, and the voice behind THE IT NOMAD on YouTube.

A&D Roster Recruitment

We are currently looking for serious Delta Force Mobile Attack & Defend players. Read the requirements, apply through Discord, and come ready to compete.

Requirements

  • Team-first mindset
  • Positive attitude and maturity
  • Active A&D player
  • Microphone required
  • Good communication and callouts
  • Reliable and consistent
  • Willing to learn, improve, and play as a team

Not Required

  • Perfect K/D ratio
  • Top leaderboard ranking
  • Insane kill counts

Zero Tolerance

  • Toxic behavior
  • Rage quitting
  • Cheating or exploiting
  • Disrespect toward teammates or the community
  • Constant blaming or complaining
  • Refusing to play the objective
